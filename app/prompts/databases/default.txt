IMPORTANT DATABASE-SPECIFIC INSTRUCTIONS:
- Always use standard PostgreSQL syntax
- Ensure queries are well-structured and optimized
- Keep queries focused and efficient - avoid selecting unnecessary columns
- Always escape SQL reserved keywords used as column names with double quotes
- Common reserved words: "end", "order", "user", "group", "limit", "offset", "select", "from", "where", "join"
- Use appropriate JOINs when querying multiple tables
- Include comments in complex queries to explain your reasoning
- For date comparisons, use CURRENT_DATE or CURRENT_TIMESTAMP
